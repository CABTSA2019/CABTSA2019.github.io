Jazz at its finest

The Bisque of the Pronghorn an American brass band, that performs many Jazz standards, and some pop using instruments such as the trombone, trumpet, sax, and much more! On this site, you can find music, merchandise, and business inquiries with the band.

ABOUT

The Bisque of the Pronghorn is a band well known for their interesting usage of the brass instruments, and revival of the jazz genre in brass. Some even say that the Bisque of the Pronghorn has put the music, back in music. Duke’s sax actually won an award from the Sax Convention in 2017 for Sax. Louis Ellington rings of an old soul, and has been compared to some of the Jazz greats. The band is in the running for a Grammy, as their awe-inspiring album, “Antilocapra Americana” has received a nomination for the best Album of the Year.
