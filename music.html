The band has performed many Jazz standards such as Autumn Leaves, Georgia, and There Will Never Be Another You. One of their more notable pop-standards is Uptown Funk. Below you can find two of their most notable original albums, What Did I Do Prong? and Antilocapra Americana.

ALBUMS

The band's debut album What Did I Do Prong? remains to this day an essential in their discography, combining elements of soul, funk and jazz.

Antilocapra Americana is the culmination of the decades of musical expertise built up by the fellow instrumentalists, and is likely to keep you hooked for weeks.
