Where does the future lie for Delaware EV?

NEW VEHICLE

We are proud to announce a new addition to our expanding fleet of electric vehicles: The Electricycle. Glide down roads in sleek style, with a powerful A/C engine fueled by cutting edge lithium-ion batteries. The lack of liquids and a gasoline motor makes for a smoother and subtler ride, all while giving the environment some much needed help. We hope that the Electricycle can provide motorcyclists an exciting gateway into the future of electric vehicles.

INTRODUCING THE ELECTRICYLCE

The Electricycle begins shipping August 2020, starting at $10,000.

DelEV's Future

At Delaware EV, our ethos is one of constant innovation and improvement. When it comes to our vehicles our only plan is to continue to make cheaper, safer, and more efficient transportation. We see EVs as the way of a sustainable, zero-emissions future, so we’re committed to seeing that through. To accomplish this we reinvest most of our own profits into research and development as well as donations to environmental groups. In addition, we hope to announce a new vehicle and updates to our existing lines in 2021.
