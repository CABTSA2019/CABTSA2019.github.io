(Automated) Self-Driving


The Model 1 comes equipped with state-of-the-art self-driving capabilities, for purposes of both comfort and safety. When self-driving is turned on, the driver can safely take their hands off the wheel and their mind off the road as the Model 1 traverses rural roads, highways and even inner city streets completely independently.


360 sensor coverage informs the internal computer of its surroundings, processing road signs, obstacles and potential dangers. Combined with an internal GPS, you can configure your destination and route and set out, all with minimal input.


Self-driving is made possible through advancements in digital image-recognition that didn’t exist only a few years ago, so you can be sure that you’re sitting in the cutting edge of technology when you’re driving the Model 1.


Safety being one of the highest goals of our company, we would only allow this kind of functionality if it meant a safer drive for everyone on the road. When self-driving is on, your risk of a crash is reduced significantly, as long as you remain vigilant and attentive to the road as the vehicle operates.

DelEV App


By downloading the DelEV app and inputting your model number/Vehicle Identification Number you can connect your car to your phone and unlock a slew of information and interactions for your vehicle.


With DelEV you can control when your vehicle charges, allowing you to charge the Model 1 when electricity is cheaper, as well as manually stopping and starting your charge without having to unplug it.


Control heating, wipers and locks to warm up your car in the morning, or open the back door with a push of a button after a shopping trip.

Wifi Hotspot


A built in wifi hotspot makes the Model 1 a media center on wheels, enabling the use of the internet anywhere on the road. On a road trip everyone can choose the entertainment on their own devices, so no one has to endure the dullness of the road.


Setting this feature up through an internet service provider will allow you to choose a plan that suits you and your family, from 1gb to unlimited.


Additionally, this connectivity allows you to remotely start your car with the [APP NAME], as well as see real time information on your car’s condition and charge.

As with all our cars, the lack of a regular combustion engine allows us to include a larger “crumple zone” in the vehicle which reduces the force delivered to a passenger in the event of a frontal impact. Our sedan model also has the added safety feature of an energy absorbing sill structure, which helps protect passengers in the event of a side impact. Our sedan also has the lowest roll over rate of any of our cars, since it not only has our low center of gravity design all our cars have, but it’s short structure and strong structure reduces this rate even further.
