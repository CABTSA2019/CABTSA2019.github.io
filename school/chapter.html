MOTTO/CREED

"Learning to lead in a technical world."

Our Chapter proudly upholds the Technology Student Organization’s creed, with all of our members committed to learning the technical and leadership skills necessary for success in today’s world.

ABOUT

OUR HISTORY

Because of a large interest in technology, the High School Communication Arts teacher worked hard to meet the interests of students and in 2010, our TSA Chapter was founded. In 2011, we made it all the way to Nationals. Unfortunately we didn't place in anything but members enjoyed the experience and the opportunity. TSA allowed them to create strong bonds with other peers that they would have never imagined and to meet and make new friends at Nationals in Orlando, Florida.

Since then we have attended nationals every year and expanded our yearly member count to about 30.

FUNDRAISING

FUNDRAISING

Bizarre Bazaar- In partnership with our school’s middle level TSA chapter, we held a craft fair called the ‘Bizarre Bazaar’ on Saturday, December 7th. Local businesses were invited to sell their goods, helping to raise money and awareness for TSA.

Pizza Fundraiser- Introduced this year, this fundraiser has volunteers from our chapter selling hot pizza to hungry students after school, which has proven successful especially when school shows and events are happening.

Snack Shack- Students from our chapter are also encouraged to volunteer at the Snack Shack in the lobby after school from which a portion of profits is given to TSA.
