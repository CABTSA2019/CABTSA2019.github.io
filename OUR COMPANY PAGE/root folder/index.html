Carbon Impact-

The carbon footprint of electric cars is up to 40% lower than gasoline-powered cars and our vehicles have up to 70% less carbon footprint because of our manufacturing techniques. In all of our manufacturing facilities, we use carbon neutral power sources such as solar and wind. Our dealership locations also run on either solar, wind, or nuclear energy, and we will never use coal within our manufacturing processes. We inform our drivers about the best places to charge, where the electricity is generated from carbon neutral, renewable sources. Our drivers are changing the world for the better.
