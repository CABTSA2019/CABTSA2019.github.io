Mission

Environment-

Our company believes in changing our world one action at a time. The environment and providing a clean method of transportation for ordinary people is our goal. Electric vehicles have no combustion and no tailpipe emissions, so our customers donâ€™t have to worry about their impact on air pollution. In the U.S. alone, transportation makes up 29% of total greenhouse gas emissions, and one of our main goals as a company is to be able to provide an Earth-friendly transportation option.

Safe/Affordable Transportation-

Our other primary goal is to have safe vehicles and to cater to people of all incomes and socio-economic statuses. Our electric vehicles are for everyone. With three models and more in development, our mission is to aid people in creating a more eco-conscious world.

Accomplishments

Carbon Impact-

The carbon footprint of electric cars is up to 40% lower than gasoline-powered cars and our vehicles have up to 70% less carbon footprint because of our manufacturing techniques. In all of our manufacturing facilities, we use carbon neutral power sources such as solar and wind. Our dealership locations also run on either solar, wind, or nuclear energy, and we will never use coal within our manufacturing processes. We inform our drivers about the best places to charge, where the electricity is generated from carbon neutral, renewable sources. Our drivers are changing the world for the better.

Innovations-

At Delaware EV, we have worked tirelessly to push the technology of electric vehicles forwards in terms of efficiency and cost-effectiveness. Thanks to our investments in lithium-ion battery research, we have been able to produce cars with greater range for cheaper. In addition, enabling self-driving in our vehicles has helped us to gather valuable data and push an emerging technology forwards.

Donations-

We donate a portion of our profits to environmental advocacy organizations such as the World Wildlife Fund and the Climateworks Foundation. As of 2019, we have contributed more than one million dollars in total.
